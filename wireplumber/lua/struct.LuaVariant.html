Struct wireplumber::lua::LuaVariant

source · 
#[repr(transparent)]
pub struct LuaVariant<'a>(_);
Expand description

A [Variant] that can be passed to a Lua script.

LuaVariant serves as a sort of adhoc ABI for WirePlumber modules and their configuration data.
